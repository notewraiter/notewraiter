What It Is

notewrAIter is a ground-breaking solution to one of medicine's most prevalent problems: writing notes.

Our service simplifies and automates specific parts of the documentation process and saves valuable time for you and your team.

Our technology is designed to accurately capture the relevant details from each patient visit. Using a customizable template, that information is rapidly filled in to a note that can easily be edited and uploaded to your EHR.

We're here to revolutionize healthcare documentation, one note at a time.

How It Works

Our process is simple and intuitive. notewrAIter employs advanced AI algorithms to listen to your conversations with patients. These algorithms are capable of understanding and interpreting complex medical terminology and nuanced patient interactions.

As you talk, our system transcribes the conversation and translates it into comprehensive, well-structured clinical notes. The AI ensures that key details are captured accurately and consistently.

Our AI has been trained on a vast dataset of medical conversations and documentation, enabling it to understand a wide range of medical specialties and conditions. It can adapt to individual provider styles and preferences for a truly personalized experience.

Finally, our system integrates seamlessly with your existing Electronic Health Record (EHR) system, making it easy to review, edit, and approve notes before they are saved to a patient's record.

With notewrAIter, you can focus on your patients while we handle the documentation.

Services We Offer

At notewrAIter, we offer a variety of services to cater to the diverse needs of healthcare providers. Our primary service is our AI-powered note-taking website, which can be accessed from your computer or mobile device, wherever you are.

For providers who prefer a more integrated experience, we offer a dedicated computer and microphone setup for your office rooms. This allows you to have hands-free conversations with patients, with our AI capturing every detail.

In addition to our core offerings, we also provide training and support to ensure a smooth transition for your practice. Our team of experts will guide you through the setup process, and provide ongoing support as you use our system.

We are also committed to maintaining the highest standards of data security and patient privacy. All data processed by notewrAIter is encrypted and stored securely, in compliance with HIPAA regulations.
