What It Is

NoteWriter is a ground-breaking solution to one of medicine's most widespread burdens: writing notes.

Doctors and other healthcare providers spend, on average, 2-3 hours per day writing notes from their patient visits.

Our service simplifies and automates specific parts of the documentation process and saves valuable time for you and your team.

How It Works

NoteWriter is a simple solution to a complex problem.

Our technology is designed to accurately capture the relevant details from each patient visit. Using a customizable template, that information is rapidly filled in to a note that you can easily edit and add to your current EHR.

With NoteWriter, you can listen more to your patients and less on writing a note.

Services We Offer

Our primary service is our AI-powered note-taking website, which can be accessed from your computer or mobile device, wherever you are.

For providers who prefer a more integrated experience, we offer a dedicated computer and microphone setup for your office rooms. This allows you to have hands-free conversations with patients.

In addition to our core offerings, we also provide training and support to ensure a smooth transition for your practice. Our team of experts will guide you through the setup process, and provide ongoing support as you use our system.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data security and patient privacy. All data processed by NoteWriter is encrypted. What we are required to keep is stored securely, in compliance with HIPAA regulations.
