What It Is

NoteWriter is a ground-breaking solution to one of healthcare's most prevalent problems: writing notes.

Healthcare providers today spend 45% of their time using Electronic Health Records (EHRs). A significant portion of that time is spent documenting interactions with patients into notes.

NoteWriter uses Artificial Intelligence to simplify and automate specific parts of the documentation process, saving hours of valuable time for you and your team.

How It Works

Our technology accurately captures the relevant medical details from each patient visit. Using customizable templates, those details are rapidly written into a note that can easily be edited and uploaded to any EHR.

With NoteWriter, you can focus on your patients and leave the (medical) details to us.

Services We Offer

Our primary service will be our AI-powered note-taking website, which can be accessed from your computer or mobile device, wherever you are.

For providers who prefer a more integrated experience, we offer a dedicated computer and microphone setup for your office rooms. This allows you to have hands-free conversations with patients, without having to worry about holding a phone or laptop.

In addition to these core offerings, we provide training and support to ensure a smooth transition for your practice. Our team of experts will guide you through the setup process, and provide ongoing support as you use our system.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data security and patient privacy. All data processed by NoteWriter is encrypted. Any data that must be stored is held securely and in compliance with HIPAA regulations.
